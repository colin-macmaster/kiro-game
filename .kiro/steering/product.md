# Product Overview

**Super Kiro World** is a browser-based 2D platformer game built for the AWS Re:Invent workshop. The game features side-scrolling gameplay where players control a character (using the Kiro logo as a sprite) to navigate platforms, collect crowns, and reach the goal flag.

## Core Gameplay
- Side-scrolling platformer with physics-based movement
- Collect crowns for points (5 points each)
- Navigate across platforms with jumping mechanics
- Reach the goal flag to complete the level
- Lives system (3 lives, lose a life when falling off the world)
- Score tracking throughout gameplay

## Target Audience
Workshop participants learning game development with AI assistance, suitable for developers of all skill levels building their first game or experimenting with game mechanics.
